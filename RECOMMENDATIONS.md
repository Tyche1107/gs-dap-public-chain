# What I Would Do If I Were Goldman Sachs GS DAP Leadership

**Context:** GS DAP on Canton Network: $500M TVL, 3 clients, rumored $500M spin-out valuation. Public RWA market: $1.85B across 9+ protocols. GS DAP captured 21% of total market but locked out of 79% by choosing permissioned infrastructure.

---

## Immediate Actions (0-3 Months)

### 1. Accept the Niche Positioning
**Hard Truth:** GS DAP is not going to win the broad RWA market.

**Why:**
- Canton Network is permissioned → Can only serve ultra-large clients
- Public RWA protocols (Ondo, Centrifuge, Maple) already captured $1.85B
- Network effects favor first-movers on public chains
- Can't pivot to permissionless without abandoning existing clients

**Action:**
- Internally: Stop comparing to Ondo/Maple (different market)
- Externally: Rebrand as "Ultra-Large Institutional RWA Platform"
- Investors: Set expectations for client count (3-10, not 1000+)

**Messaging:**
- "GS DAP serves government and supranational entities"
- "Average deal size: $167M (vs $10M-$50M for public RWA)"
- "We're the Goldman Sachs of tokenized assets—premium, exclusive"

---

**Current Problem:** $500M valuation assumes massive growth potential.

**Global Ultra-Large RWA Potential:**
- Government bonds eligible for tokenization: ~$10T

- GS DAP's potential market share: 10-20%
- TAM for GS DAP: $10B-$20B

**Math:**
- Current: $500M (3 clients)

**Action:**
- Publish TAM analysis (convince spin-out investors)

**Canton Network Expansion:**

**b) Multi-Product Canton**
- Don't just tokenize bonds
- GS DAP can expand:
  - Equity tokens (private company shares)
  - Commodity tokens (gold, oil)

- 5 product categories on Canton by end of 2026

**Controversial Take:** GS DAP should launch BOTH permissioned and permissionless products.

**Why:**
- Canton (permissioned) serves ultra-large ($100M+)
- Public chain (permissionless) serves institutional ($1M-$50M)
- Different clients, different products, different brands

**How to Execute:**

**Canton (Existing):**
- Brand: "GS DAP"

- Products: Bonds, large-scale securitizations

**Why This Works:**
- Doesn't cannibalize Canton (different client segments)
- Captures $1.85B public RWA market
- Leverages Goldman brand in both markets
- Reduces spin-out risk (diversified revenue)

**Timeline:**
- Q2 2026: Announce "Goldman RWA Public Product" strategy
- Q3 2026: Launch first permissionless token (e.g., "GS Emerging Market Bond Token")
- Q4 2026: $100M-$500M TVL on public chains

## Long-Term Vision (12-24 Months)

### 7. Become the "Stripe of Tokenized Assets"
**Current:** GS DAP is a product (tokenized bonds on Canton).

**Vision:** GS DAP becomes infrastructure (other issuers build on Canton).

**Business Model Shift:**

**From:** Goldman tokenizes assets for clients  
**To:** Goldman provides Canton infrastructure, clients tokenize themselves

**Revenue Model:**
- Platform fees (0.1-0.5% of TVL)
- Transaction fees (5-10 bps per trade)
- SaaS licensing (banks pay to use Canton)

**Example:**
- Current: EIB pays Goldman to tokenize $100M bond → One-time fee
- Future: EIB uses Canton to tokenize EVERY bond → Recurring revenue

**Why This Matters:**
- Scalability (don't need GS salespeople for every deal)
- Recurring revenue (SaaS multiples > one-time deals)
- Defensibility (once institutions build on Canton, hard to switch)

## What NOT to Do

### ❌ Don't Try to Compete with Public RWA on Their Turf
**Temptation:** "We should make Canton permissionless to capture $1.85B!"

**Why Not:**
- Existing 3 clients chose Canton BECAUSE it's permissioned
- Opening Canton → clients leave → valuation collapses
- Can't out-DeFi Ondo/Centrifuge (they're native, you're not)

**Alternative:**
- Accept niche OR launch separate public product (dual-track)
- Don't destroy Canton trying to chase public market

### ❌ Don't Overpromise on Spin-Out Valuation
**Current Rumor:** $500M valuation

**Reality:** 1.00x TVL multiple at 3 clients is already generous.

**Risk:**
- Promise $500M → Deliver $200M → Investor lawsuits
- Promise 50 clients → Deliver 10 → "Growth story" collapses

**Better:**
- Under-promise: "$300M-$500M range depending on client pipeline"
- Over-deliver: Hit $600M by adding 5 new clients

### ❌ Don't Spin Out Too Early
**Goldman's Advantage:** Brand, balance sheet, regulatory licenses

**Post-Spin-Out:**
- Separate company
- Less brand equity ("GS DAP" vs "Goldman Sachs")
- Harder to attract ultra-large clients (who want Goldman directly)

**Recommendation:**
- Stay within Goldman for 2-3 more years
- Hit $2B-$5B TVL
- Then spin out with proven track record
- Valuation will be higher ($1B-$2B vs $500M now)
